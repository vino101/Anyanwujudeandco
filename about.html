About Our Firm

At Anyanwu Jude & Co., we're an indigenous firm of Chartered Accountants dedicated to delivering top-notch accounting, audit, tax, training, outsourcing, and accounting software consultancy services. Based in Lagos, we were incorporated in June 2009 with a commitment to providing quality and value-added services that meet the evolving needs of our clients across all sectors of the economy.

Our Clients

At Anyanwu Jude & Co, we serve a diverse range of clients, from individuals to corporate organizations, across various sectors of the economy. Our expertise spans multiple areas, including:

Auditing and assurance services, Tax planning and compliance, Financial consultancy and advisory services, Accounting and bookkeeping services, Outsourcing services, Forensic accounting and investigation, Corporate Affairs Commission (CAC) services, Project appraisals, supervision, and monitoring, Financial training and capacity building.

We also have specific experience in auditing Joint Ventures (JV) Agreements and Production Sharing Contract (PSC) Agreements, and we're committed to delivering high-quality services that meet the unique needs of our clients.

Operational Philosophy

We would like to state that our operational philosophy has been a carry-over of the professional standards of integrity, ethics, competence and strive for excellence, acquired from professional trainings at Chuba Medidem & Co.

Basically, our operating policies are guided by the following principles: -

  • We appreciate clients of high integrity who recognize the need for and require high standards of financial reporting and advice.
  • We recognize that clients' service is the root of our professional practice and we endeavor to maintain appropriate levels of efficiency and technical support to effectively meet the needs of each client.
  • Our work is done after careful reviews and adequate planning, and is backed with efficient procedures, proper supervision and quality control reviews which ensure maintenance of high standards.
  • Our reports are usually clear, constructive and forward looking, intending only to promote efficiency and progress in the client organisations. In this regard also, we endeavor to respect the sensitivities of each organization and take steps to ensure maximum confidentiality for each client's affairs.

Our Fee Structure

Our fees are usually based on the degree of responsibility and skill involved, and the time necessarily occupied on the work, charged at hourly rates within ranges approved by the Institute of Chartered Accountants of Nigeria. We are also prepared to discuss with the parties concerned, in order to negotiate mutually beneficial arrangements regarding fees and re-imburseables, or to abide by any pre-determined fee regime where such exits.

Standard Services

Charged at hourly rates within approved ranges, based on responsibility and skill involved.

Negotiable Arrangements

We discuss mutually beneficial terms, including re-imbursables and pre-determined regimes.
